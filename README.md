# FirstStep Founder Platform

A pre-startup founder readiness platform that helps first-time entrepreneurs move from an idea or skill to a clear first action, verified support, early customer opportunities, and investor readiness.

A static website prototype for Challenge 1 — Taking the First Entrepreneurial Step.

## Project Overview

FirstStep is designed for people who have a business idea, useful skill, or small business opportunity but do not know where to begin. Many first-time founders are not blocked by lack of ambition; they are blocked by confusion around licensing, costs, documents, funding, customer validation, and trusted connections.

This platform acts as the preparation layer before founders enter larger startup ecosystems, accelerators, government programs, or investor networks. It helps users become structured, verified, and ready to take meaningful action.

## Problem Statement

Many people in the community have viable ideas or real skills, but they never start because they do not know:

- What their first move should be
- What documents they need
- How much it may cost to start
- Which licenses or approvals may be required
- How to validate their idea
- How much investment to ask for
- How to safely approach mentors, investors, or corporate partners

FirstStep solves this by turning an unclear idea into a guided founder journey.

## Solution

FirstStep provides a digital platform where first-time founders can:

- Create a founder profile
- Receive a business readiness score
- Generate a step-by-step action plan
- Estimate startup costs
- Understand possible license requirements
- Validate their idea with potential customers
- Prepare investor documents
- Explain how much investment they need
- Connect with mentors and verified investors
- Apply for corporate pilot opportunities
- Use secure agreement forms before sharing sensitive information
- Track progress from idea to launch

## Why It Is Different From Hub71

Hub71 and similar startup ecosystems usually support startups that are already formed, ready to grow, or prepared to access investors and markets.

FirstStep focuses on the earlier stage: people who are still at the beginning and need help becoming ready.

FirstStep does not replace ecosystems like Hub71. Instead, it prepares beginner founders so they can later enter accelerators, government programs, investor networks, or startup ecosystems with stronger profiles, clearer documents, and better traction.

In simple terms:

- **Hub71 helps startups scale. FirstStep helps people start.**

## Corporate Pilot Marketplace

The Corporate Pilot Marketplace connects first-time founders with companies, government entities, and organizations that are looking for real solutions, products, or services.

Instead of only helping founders prepare documents or speak to investors, this feature helps them access their first real business opportunity. Corporate partners can post pilot challenges, and founders can apply with their proposed solution, timeline, cost estimate, and business profile.

This helps founders gain early traction, customer feedback, revenue opportunities, and proof of demand before approaching investors or larger startup ecosystems.
